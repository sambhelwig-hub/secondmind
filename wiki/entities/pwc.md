# PwC

## Overview

One of the "Big Four" professional services and consulting firms. Collectively, the Big Four (PwC, KPMG, EY, Deloitte) employ over 1 million people globally.

## Key Details

- **Services:** Auditing, tax advisory, transaction services, M&A due diligence, compliance, risk management
- **Career path:** Consultant → Senior Consultant → Manager → Senior Manager → Director → Partner
- **Notable for:** Transaction Services unit, which handles M&A due diligence and advisory

## Relevance to Wiki

Represents major career path in financial services and consulting. [[ned|Ned]] started his career in PwC's Transaction Services unit.
