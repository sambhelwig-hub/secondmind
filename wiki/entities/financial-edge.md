# Financial Edge

## Overview

Finance education and training company providing online courses, certifications, and resources for finance professionals. Known for practical, industry-focused training on financial modeling, investment banking, private equity, and other finance career paths.

## Key Details

- Offers online courses and certifications (Micro-degrees)
- Provides public in-person training courses
- Corporate training programs for financial institutions
- Free resources including blogs, guides, and tutorials
- "Felix" learning platform for online courses
