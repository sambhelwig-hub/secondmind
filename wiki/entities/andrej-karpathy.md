# Andrej Karpathy

## Overview

AI researcher and educator. Former director of AI at Tesla, co-founder of OpenAI. Known for making deep learning accessible through lectures and tutorials. Author of the [[llm-wiki-pattern]] described in [[karpathy-llm-wiki|his gist]].

## Key Details

- Proposed the LLM Wiki pattern as a way to build personal knowledge bases with LLMs as active maintainers rather than passive retrievers.
- Uses Obsidian as the browsing interface alongside an LLM agent.

## Relevance to Wiki

Author of the founding document for this wiki's architecture. The structural decisions in [[CLAUDE.md]] trace directly to his pattern.
