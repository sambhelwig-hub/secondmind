# Ned

## Overview

Founder and CEO of 365 Financial Analyst, an online education platform that has helped over 3 million people worldwide acquire career-enhancing finance skills. Finance graduate with experience across multiple finance domains.

## Key Details

- **Background:** Finance graduate with experience as a forex dealer, financial analyst, M&A advisor, in-house M&A analyst, and financial consultant
- **Started career:** PwC's Transaction Services unit
- **Platform:** 365financialanalyst.com
- **Mission:** Address the gap between university finance education and real-world industry requirements by teaching practical skills (Excel, financial modeling, accounting, business strategy)
