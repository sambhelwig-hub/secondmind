# Vannevar Bush

## Overview

American engineer, inventor, and science administrator. In 1945, published "As We May Think" in The Atlantic, describing the [[memex]] — a hypothetical personal device for storing, annotating, and linking documents with associative trails.

## Key Details

- The Memex is widely considered a conceptual precursor to hypertext and the World Wide Web.
- Bush's vision was private and actively curated — closer to the [[llm-wiki-pattern]] than to what the public web became.
- The unsolved piece of the Memex was maintenance: who builds and keeps the associative trails current? The LLM Wiki pattern answers this with LLMs.

## Relevance to Wiki

Historical antecedent for the entire approach. The [[llm-wiki-pattern]] is a modern realization of his 1945 vision, with LLMs solving the maintenance problem.
