# Financial Services and Consulting

## Definition

Advisory and professional services firms that assist businesses with finance-related challenges: M&A, restructuring, auditing, tax, compliance, risk management, and performance improvement.

## Explanation

Thousands of financial consulting firms worldwide provide these services. The "Big Four" — [[pwc]], [[kpmg]], [[ey]], and [[deloitte]] — collectively employ over 1 million people globally.

## Major Service Lines

**Auditing**
- Auditors collaborate with client companies to verify financial statements are accurate and reflect true financial status
- Develops outstanding technical and accounting skills
- Foundation role for many consultants

**Transaction Services**
- Due diligence for M&A (investigating target companies)
- M&A advisory
- Tax advisory
- Performance improvement

## Career Progression in Consulting

Consultant → Senior Consultant/Executive → Assistant Manager → Manager → Senior Manager → Director → Associate Partner → Partner

(This illustrates the variety of title hierarchies across consulting firms.)

## Appeal

[[ned|Ned]] began his career in PwC's Transaction Services unit and found it "enriching." He was able to:
- Work on many deals
- Hone technical skills (Excel, accounting, financial analysis)
- Evaluate business strategies of numerous companies
- Engage directly with entrepreneurs and finance directors
